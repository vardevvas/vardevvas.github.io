The War

I think everyone knew that a crisis was about to hit, but no one was ready for an actual war, when we heard the first explosion we were shocked to our core, I mean you see it and hear it in the movies and while you are playing games but in real life, when you are in it, it’s something else entirely of course when it started, everyone was being optimistic that it’ll all be over in a matter of hours, with no electricity, water or fuel it was almost like being captive. one funny note was the realization that there isn’t gonna be work tomorrow after a week in the midest of this we found a way to leave the city, and me being stupidly optimistic thinking that it’ll all be over in about two weeks, took only two sets of clothes leaving everything behind, which later we found that it was all raveged I’ve lost a lot, we all did, but.. getting back up is the only option I have, to help me and my family, that’s why I moved to UAE to find a way for a new life, a better life.
